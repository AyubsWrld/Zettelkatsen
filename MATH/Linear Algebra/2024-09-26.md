#### Ex. Subspace or not a Subspace
____

\begin{bmatrix} 
	x\\
	y\\
	2x + 3y\\
	\end{bmatrix}
$$ is a subspace of $R^3$ . 
$$ W =
\begin{bmatrix} 
	x\\
	y\\
	2x + 3y + 1\\
	\end{bmatrix}
$$ is not a subspace of $R^3$ . 

$$ T =
\begin{bmatrix} 
	a + bt + (a+b)t^2\\
